title:

water nymph

date:

10/16/2019

medium:

.jpg / AR (augmented reality)

description:

a water nymph (inspired by the little mermaid, ariel) relaxes in the ocean, drifting off into a deep sleep.

. . .

instructions:

1. open blippar app

2. input test code "1301363"

3. scan (note: the video may take a while to load).

process:

i created the .jpg file on PhotoShop using my stylus for my 2-in-1 Dell laptop. i then gathered a "water rippling" video (courtesy of ChoiceSlides on YouTube, link @ https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zFGyMUNinSM), and used it as an overlay in PremierePro with the opacity at about 40%. to create the AR, in BlippAR, i used the original .jpg file as a marker, and placed the video above it as a widget. i also added a button to play audio ("The Finish Line" by Middle School) to add onto the "vibe" of the artwork.

. . .
