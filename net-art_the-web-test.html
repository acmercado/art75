the web "test"

date: 10/28/2019

this website was created to demonstrate how the internet is a source of "surveillance" as it tracks what you do based on the input information you give it. this website also embodies a few internet "trends" such as memes and asmr. in its own way, it is a form of "net art". as it allows its audience to actively interact with it. it was sort of difficult trying to figure out what to put on each page, but as I kept working with it, it turned into something very "playful" and "fun."

visit the actual page @ " https://acmercado.github.io/critical-interactivity/index.html "

all imgs, gifs, and videos were pulled from the internet. this project consists of many pages with two options that would lead to other pages. linking each page to another got kind of confusing towards the end, so i tried to keep it as simple as possible, while also still trying to convey my message. there are about 4 "endings" that one could get based on their selections.
