tomo cave

date: 10/15/2019

for this project, i wanted to create a page that focused on the cuteness of my cat, momo, and my boyfriend's cat, tora. their names combined create "tomo".

to acheieve the "cuteness" of our cats, i tried to place them in every way I could: the background, as icons, in the videos themselves, and in a poem.

visit the actual page @ " https://acmercado.github.io/video-net-art/ "

using Adobe's PhotoShop, i created each cat picture as a different layer, copied and pasted several times, changed the opacity, and set the background color to a pink tone in order to create the background of the website. i also created the "sticky" photos in Adobe's PhotoShop to make them as PNG files.

i color-corrected the videos in Adobe's Premiere Pro to make them have a pink tone in order for them to look more cohesive beside each other.

as for the music, gif, and mouse, i gathered them from online. (the playlist was made by me on my Spotify account).
